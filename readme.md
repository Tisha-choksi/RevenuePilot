# RevenuePilot

RevenuePilot is an enterprise-grade AI Sales Automation Platform built using a multi-agent architecture. It autonomously qualifies leads, understands customer requirements, recommends products, handles objections, generates quotes, schedules follow-ups, and updates CRM systems in real time.

The platform combines Large Language Models (LLMs), Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), long-term memory, tool calling, and workflow orchestration to simulate a highly efficient sales team. RevenuePilot leverages specialized AI agents for lead qualification, product recommendation, pricing, negotiation, customer engagement, and sales analytics.

## Key Features

* Multi-Agent Sales Workflow powered by LangGraph
* Intelligent Lead Qualification and Scoring
* RAG-based Product Knowledge Retrieval
* Customer Memory and Context Awareness
* Automated Objection Handling
* Dynamic Pricing and Discount Management
* Quote and Proposal Generation
* CRM Integration and Deal Tracking
* Automated Follow-up and Outreach Campaigns
* Human-in-the-Loop Escalation
* Sales Analytics and Performance Dashboard
* Tool Calling for Inventory, Pricing, Email, and Scheduling Systems

## Architecture

RevenuePilot follows an agentic architecture where multiple specialized AI agents collaborate to drive the entire sales lifecycle—from prospect discovery and qualification to conversion and customer retention.

The system is designed for scalability, observability, and enterprise deployment while maintaining high accuracy through retrieval-augmented generation, persistent memory, and tool-based reasoning.

## Goal

To build an autonomous AI sales representative capable of engaging prospects, qualifying opportunities, recommending solutions, negotiating deals, and assisting businesses in increasing revenue with minimal human intervention.
